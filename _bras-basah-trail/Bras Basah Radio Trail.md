<p>Relax, plug in and immerse yourself with our unique radio trail through
the Bras Basah district from now until September 2025.</p>
<p>Here's what to expect:&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Trail Experience:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Visit 5 locations in the Bras Basah area:</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Singapore Alcove, Central Public Library</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Art x Stacks Hub, Lee Kong Chian Reference Library Level 8</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Narrative Coffee Stand (Bras Basah Centre)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basheer Graphic Books (Bras Basah Centre)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Straits Art Co. (North Bridge Centre)</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>At each stop, enjoy a radio segment featuring:</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>• The magic of Sing Lit</p>
<p>• A day-in-a-life of a bookstore owner</p>
<p>• Book repair ASMR</p>
<p>• Cosy café moments with healing fiction</p>
<p>• Creative inspiration from books</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>How to Participate:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Find the Bras Basah Radio Trail poster at each location</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collect a newsletter from the Singapore Alcove at Central Public Library</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Complete the stamp rally by stamping the boxes in your newsletter</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Audio Experience:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Scan QR codes at each location to unlock audio tracks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Listen as you explore or relax at a cosy spot</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Gift Pack Redemption:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Show your completed newsletter on selected redemption dates in February
and March 2025 (while stocks last).</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Follow&nbsp;<strong>@readingnationsg on Instagram and Facebook</strong>&nbsp;for
updates on the trail and related events.</p>
